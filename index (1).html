All about Penguins

This website is devoted to penguins.

Why Penguins are cool

Penguins are cool because they live in the Southern Hemisphere in cooler waters. Many misconceptions about penguins are common. For example , many winter scenes have Polar Bears and Penguins in the same image when they never live together!

The claymation animation Pengu popularized penguins with Pengu's comical experiences in modern day society.

Penguin Habitats

Penguins live in Antartica, but are found throughout the southern points of South America, Africa, and Australia too!
